## Overview

A comprehensive design of a bench vice focused on functionality, durability and ease of use for clamping tasks in a workshop environment. The project covers the full CAD workflow from individual components to a complete, documented assembly.

## Scope

- **Detailed 3D modelling** of every component — base plate, jaws, screw bar, set screws and fasteners.
- **Assembly design** with proper mates and constraints to represent real motion and fit.
- **Material selection** (steel throughout) suited to the loads and wear of a clamping tool.
- **Bill of Materials (BOM)** generation listing each part, quantity and material.

## Design highlights

- **Ergonomic design** for comfortable, practical operation.
- **Structural stability** for reliable clamping under load.
- **Aesthetic considerations** balanced against function and manufacturability.

## Tools

Designed in **Fusion 360**, covering modelling, assembly, drafting and BOM generation — demonstrating an end-to-end CAD competency from concept to production-ready documentation.
